My name is Dean and I have been a qualified personal trainer since 2019. My love of fitness has always been a part of my life. So it made total sense to turn my passion for fitness training into a qualification. As well as transforming my own body through fitness training and good nutrition, I have helped many clients to redefine themselves through health & fitness training. Not only sharing my knowledge, I also share personal experiences, tips and health & fitness training secrets.

Since my Personal Training and coaching expertise is designed to help improve technique for the body, you will certainly gain better results and help prevent injury. It’s about having a stronger body but also a strong mindset. Your body is capable of so much more and I am here to break down the barriers of the mind stopping you from reaching your full potential. Every client is different and unique, with their own strengths and weaknesses. My training sessions are tailored to each individual’s need. As a result, together we will exceed your expectations. You will also find that you look forward to our sessions before, during and after our workouts.


Free Personal Fitness training consultation


Before we start training there will be a free consultation lasting around 30 mins to an hour depending on your specific requirements. The consultation is to assess your needs and what outcome you would like to have at the end of the sessions. After that, we will start the journey towards smashing your health & fitness goals!
