Personal Training Sessions


Each 60-minute session is conducted at a location convenient for you—be it a local park or your home garden within East London—providing flexibility and comfort in your training environment.


Sessions


Each 60-minute session is held at a location that suits you—local park or home garden in East London.

A personal training (PT) session typically includes:


  1. Warm-Up (5-10 min): Light cardio, mobility, and stretching.
  2. Main Workout (30-40 min): A mix of boxing (punching and footwork), weight training (bodyweight or weights), circuits (back-to-back exercises), cardio (running, skipping), and core work (planks, crunches).
  3. Cool-Down (5-10 min): Stretching exercises to relax muscles.

I will watch your form, structure your workout, and keep you motivated.
