Train, Play, Repeat: your fitness & Recreation Guide


Lloyd Park (Walthamstow)

Recently renovated and offering excellent facilities:


  1. Basic outdoor gym
  2. Tennis courts and bowling greens
  3. Wide open spaces perfect for circuit training
  4. Excellent children's playground with modern equipment
  5. Clean, accessible toilet facilities
  6. Beautiful gardens making it ideal for picnics
  7. Café for post-workout refreshments

Victory Park

A smaller community space with good fitness potential:


  1. Good outdoor exercise equipment, alot of bars to do dips/ pull ups
  2. Open areas suitable for bodyweight training
  3. Small football area
  4. Modest children's playground
  5. No permanent toilet facilities
  6. Limited picnic spots
  7. Well-maintained pathways for running circuits

Barking Riverside Little Bee Park

A newer addition to the area's green spaces:


  1. Open areas perfect for HIIT training
  2. Small children's playground with bee-themed equipment
  3. No toilet facilities
  4. Picnic Areas available
  5. Excellent running paths connecting to the Thames Path
  6. Limited shade, making morning or evening workouts preferable in summer
