Welcome to the Game


This is not your average workout routine. This is Repping It — a fitness challenge that takes everyday occurrences and turns them into epic workout triggers. Whether you’re watching TV, walking outside, or just chilling, your environment is now your gym. Get creative, stay alert, and move like a beast.


Game Setup


  1. Pick Your Daily Scenario (or let fate decide): Use the dice to randomly pick one or more of the following challenges each day.
  2. Track Your Reps: Use the in game tally counter. At the end of the day, add up the total reps.
  3. Finish Strong: Complete your reps at once or spread them throughout the day. The only rule is — get it done.

Warm-Up: “Ignite the Engine”


Always warm up first to avoid injuries and get in the zone. Here’s a quick 5-minute routine:


  • 30 seconds Jumping Jacks
  • 30 seconds Arm Circles (forward + backward)
  • 30 seconds Leg Swings (each leg)
  • 30 seconds Bodyweight Squats
  • 1 minute Jog in Place
  • 2 minutes Dynamic Stretching (hip openers, lunges with reach, toe touches)

Stretch it Out: “Cooldown Flow”


After completing your reps, cool down and stretch to reduce soreness:


  • Forward Fold
  • Hip Flexor Stretch
  • Shoulder Cross Stretch
  • Seated Twist
  • Cat-Cow Stretch (yoga style)
  • Deep Breathing for 2 minutes

Final Word


Repping It isn’t just a workout — it’s a lifestyle challenge. You’ll never watch TV, walk to the shop, or scroll through YouTube the same way again. Every moment becomes an opportunity to move, laugh, and get stronger.


Get your reps in. No excuses. Go Beast Mode.
